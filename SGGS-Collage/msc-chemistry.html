Why Choose M.Sc. Chemistry?

M.Sc. Chemistry at SGGS Khalsa College Mahilpur equips students with advanced knowledge in organic, inorganic, and physical chemistry, fostering analytical, laboratory, and research skills required for modern careers.

  • Experienced Faculty & Research-Oriented Teaching
  • Well-Equipped Chemistry Labs & Instrumentation
  • Preparation for Competitive Exams: NET, GATE, CSIR
  • Foundation for Ph.D. & Higher Studies
  • Career Opportunities in Teaching, Research, and Industry

M.Sc. Chemistry – Overview

The M.Sc. Chemistry program is a 2-year postgraduate course (4 semesters) focused on advanced topics in organic, inorganic, physical, and analytical chemistry. Students learn through a mix of theoretical and practical sessions.

Career Opportunities: Graduates can work as Chemists, Researchers, Lab Analysts, or pursue Ph.D.

Admission

Admissions are open for students with a B.Sc. in Chemistry or related fields with minimum 50% marks.
